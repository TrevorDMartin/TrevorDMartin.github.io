Did You Know?

  • The earliest known popsicle's date as far back as 1872. Back then, a popsicle was called a Hokey-Pokey.
  • Popsicle's are also known as freezer pops, ice lollies, and ice pops.
  • The world's largest popsicle was made in 1997 and was 21 feet tall.
  • Popsicles have become so popular that a popular arts and craft item is called a popsicle stick.
